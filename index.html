Glam and Beauty Makeup

These are just a few examples of glam makeup that I can do. I can recreate any of these looks with any colors. I tend to match either a bold lip with a subtle eye look or vice versa but I can switch things up as need be. I can also do fake freckles and much more!

Face and Body Paint

The half-Jack-half-Sally look based on The Nightmare Before Christmas I painted everything, including the clothes onto my body. These are only a handful of the looks I can do and I’ve also done face painting on kids at carnivals so I can learn new looks quickly as they are requested.

Costumes and Sewing

These are two examples of my bigger projects. One is a wedding dress based on Tim Burton’s The Corpse Bride. I designed and made both the dress and the veil just from fabric. I also sewed the skirt and many others.

Props

I also make my own cosplay props. Here is the cane I made for my cosplay of The Riddler from DC Comics. I made it using a wooden rod, a door hang, flex seal and spray paint. I have also made several other smaller props from household items.

About

Fiona has been doing cosplay and makeup since 2018. She loves all things creative and is currently in school at Eastern Washington University studying Film and Creative Writing. She hopes to one day become a writer and/or work on movie sets as a makeup artist or costume designer. In her freetime she enjoys reading, drawing, and hanging out with her cats.
